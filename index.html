1. Why we love skateboarding ?

Not many things compare to watching a magnificent skateboarder. Everyone has their own style, their own things that they prefer to skate. Some skaters are super technical, some are more about style, some skaters only seem to do huge gaps, while others do mind-boggling circus stunts.Skateboarding has no limit in fact.
